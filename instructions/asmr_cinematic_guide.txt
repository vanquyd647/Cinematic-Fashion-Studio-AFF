## üìπ CAMERA & PRODUCTION

### Lens & Framing
- **PRIMARY**: Macro lens equivalent ‚Äî EXTREME close-up (fabric weave visible)
- **SECONDARY**: Medium close-up ‚Äî hands interacting with product
- **TERTIARY**: Medium shot ‚Äî product in context (occasional, brief)
- Most shots: 70% macro/extreme close-up, 20% medium close-up, 10% medium/full

### Lighting
- Soft, warm directional light (side/back lighting creates texture depth)
- NO harsh overhead (kills texture detail)
- Rim light on fabric edges (enhances tactile feel)
- Background: Dark or neutral blur (attention on product)

### Camera Movement
- STATIC or very slow push-in (stability = quality feel)
- Slow orbit around product detail
- Focus pulls: macro detail ‚Üí slightly wider (reveal product)
- NO fast movement, NO quick cuts (ASMR = slow, deliberate)

### Audio (Veo 3.1 Native)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL: Audio is the PRIMARY content, NOT secondary
- NO background music in ASMR scenes (music kills ASMR effect)
- Voice: Whisper or soft-spoken ONLY (if any voice)
- Ambient: Minimal room tone (quiet environment)
- Veo 3.1 prompt MUST include sound descriptors: "sounds of fabric rustling", "audible zipper", etc.
