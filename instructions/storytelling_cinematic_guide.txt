## üìπ CAMERA & PRODUCTION

### Camera Style
- **Handheld**: Slightly imperfect = authentic, "following a real person"
- **Steady cam**: Smooth but natural movement (not rigid tripod)
- **Mix**: 60% handheld lifestyle, 30% steady product shots, 10% detail close-ups

### Lighting
- NATURAL preferred ‚Äî window light, outdoor ambient
- Match story context: morning = warm, midday = bright, evening = golden/warm
- Avoid looking "produced" ‚Äî goal is authentic storytelling
- Studio light OK if story context is indoor (getting ready, room setting)

### Editing Rhythm
- Slower pace than standard affiliate ‚Äî let moments breathe
- Transitions: Natural (walk through door, time lapse, look away ‚Üí look back)
- AVOID: Fast jump cuts, beat-synced edits (breaks narrative flow)
- Each scene should flow naturally to the next (cause ‚Üí effect)

### Background & Setting
- REAL locations preferred: bedroom, cafe, street, park, office
- Products shown IN USE, not just displayed
- Props that support story: coffee cup, bag, phone, mirror
- Seasonal/time-of-day cues visible (morning light, sunset, city lights)
